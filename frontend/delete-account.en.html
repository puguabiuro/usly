USLY Account Deletion

Version1.0
Effective from8 July 2026
ControllerPUGUA Sp. z o.o.
This page explains how to delete your USLY account and the data associated with it.

1. Delete your account in the app

If you have access to the USLY app, you can delete your account directly in the app.

1) Sign in to the USLY app.

2) Go to Settings.

3) Select Delete account.

4) Confirm the deletion according to the instructions displayed in the app.

2. No access to the app

If you cannot sign in, no longer have the app installed, or have lost access to your account, you can request account deletion by email:

kontakt@uslyapp.pl

Use the subject line: USLY account deletion.

In your message, include the email address assigned to the account you want to delete.

3. Data that will be deleted

After account deletion, personal data associated with the account is deleted, in particular:

account and profile data,

profile description, interests and profile settings,

profile photos and AI avatar,

app activity data that no longer needs to be retained,

other personal data that does not need to be retained for legal, safety or support reasons.

4. Data that may be retained

Some data may be retained for a limited time where necessary to:

keep users safe,

prevent abuse,

handle reports, complaints or disputes,

comply with legal obligations.

After the required retention period ends, the data is deleted or anonymised.

5. Contact

For questions about account deletion or personal data, contact us at:

kontakt@uslyapp.pl
